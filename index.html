indianapolis

location

Indianapolis is located in the central-western part of the state of Indiana. It is in the midwest region of the USA. It runs alongside the White River.

about

history

the name indianapolis comes from the phrase "land of the indians", deriving from its indigenous population prior to english settlers. the city was founded 205 years ago in 1821. the city rose to prominence as it became a hub for trains and railroad travel because of its convenient spot in the usa.

landmarks

one major landmark in indianapolis is the indianapolis motor speedway. it is a major hub for international car racing. another landmark in indianapolis is gainbridge fieldhouse. it is home to our basketball teams and hosts big-name concerts!

culture

indianapolis has a vibrant culture. it is a blend of midwestern friendliness and a busy city life. indianapolis has a strong arts and music scene with many art exhibits and small and large music venues. there is a significant african american influence with things like jazz clubs andthe madam walker legacy center. there is also a large sports prescence with our football, basketball, and baseball stadiums in close proximity.
